Hello, my name is Mrs. DeChellis. I am in my seventh year in the library media position at Dirksen Elementary. I speak English and Polish fluently, and I enjoy working with all of the grade levels and everyone on the staff!

Our library serves Kindergarten to 8th grade students. We have many awesome books for students to check out on a weekly basis. Also, students use laptops and iPads for researching.
